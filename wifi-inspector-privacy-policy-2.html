How to Request Data Deletion

At WiFi Inspector Pro, we respect your privacy. All your network scan results, saved devices, and custom settings are stored locally on your own phone. We do not have any external servers where your personal network data is kept.

If you wish to delete your data, you can do so easily by following these steps:

  1. Delete Specific Items: Within the app, you can delete individual saved items (like Wake-on-LAN devices) by long-pressing on the device entry and selecting "Delete".
  2. Clear All App Data: You can clear everything at once through Android Settings:
    • Open phone Settings > Apps > WiFi Inspector Pro.
    • Tap on Storage.
    • Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage. This will permanently delete all local databases and scan history.
  3. Uninstall the App: When you uninstall the app from your device, Android automatically removes all local data associated with the app.

Third-Party Services (AdMob & Crashlytics)

Our app uses Google AdMob for ads and Firebase Crashlytics for bug reporting. These services use device identifiers. You can manage or reset your Advertising ID through your device's Google Settings > Ads.
