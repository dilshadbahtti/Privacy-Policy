Privacy Policy
Last Updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Muhammad Dilshad Bhatti ("we," "our," or "us") handles user data in the My Calculator Pro application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our app, My Calculator Pro, is designed to assist users with business billing, quotations, and various utility calculations. To provide these features, the app collects and processes certain information provided voluntarily by the user:

  • Personal Information: We collect Names, Phone Numbers, Email addresses, and Physical Addresses provided by the user through our billing, quotation, and contact forms.
  • Media and Files: The app accesses the Camera and Gallery for document scanning and generating PDF reports.
  • Location: The app accesses Precise Location (GPS) only when the Speed Tracker feature is active to calculate speed and distance.
  • Search History: The app processes in-app search history to help users retrieve their locally saved records.

⚠️ Important: All the above information is stored and processed strictly locally on the user's device. We do not transmit, sell, share, or upload any of this personal data to our servers or any third-party databases.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party SDKs to improve app functionality and provide free services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect Device IDs and other identifiers to serve personalized ads.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to monitor app performance and diagnose crashes. Crashlytics may collect device information to help us fix bugs.

These services collect data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control their data collection practices.

3. Data Control and Deletion

Since all user data (Name, Phone, Address, Scanned Images, and Records) is stored locally on your device:

  • Users have full control over their data.
  • You can manually delete or "Clear All" your saved records, bills, and images directly from within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app will remove the locally stored data from your device.
