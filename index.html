Data Privacy

At My Calculator Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our application.

Local Data Storage

All your calculations, saved results, and custom settings are stored locally on your own device. We do not have any external servers where your calculation history is kept. Your information remains under your complete control at all times.

How to Request Data Deletion

We understand that you may want to delete your data at any time. Below are the various methods you can use to remove your information from the app:

Method 1: Delete Specific Calculations

Within the My Calculator Pro app, you can delete individual saved calculations:

  1. Long-press on any saved calculation in your history
  2. Select "Delete" from the context menu
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Method 2: Clear All App Data

To delete all your data at once, you can clear everything through your Android device settings:

  1. Open your phone's Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps or Application Manager
  3. Find and select My Calculator Pro
  4. Tap on Storage
  5. Select Clear Data or Clear Storage
  6. Confirm your action

Note: This will permanently delete all calculation history and custom settings associated with the app.

Method 3: Uninstall the App

When you uninstall My Calculator Pro from your device, Android automatically removes all local data associated with the app. Simply:

  1. Long-press the app icon on your home screen or app drawer
  2. Select Uninstall
  3. Confirm the uninstallation

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

My Calculator Pro uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the application. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad personalization and targeting purposes.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability and fix bugs. Crashlytics may collect device identifiers and crash data to help us improve the app's performance and reliability.

Managing Your Advertising ID

You can manage or reset your Advertising ID at any time through your Android device settings:

  1. Open your phone's Settings
  2. Go to Google or Google Account
  3. Select Manage your Google Account
  4. Navigate to the Data & Privacy tab
  5. Scroll to Ads & personalization
  6. Tap Manage your Google Ads settings to opt out or reset your ID

Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • No tracking: We do not track your personal behavior or location
  • Advertising control: Manage your advertising preferences through Google settings
