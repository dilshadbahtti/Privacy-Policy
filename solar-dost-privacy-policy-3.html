Privacy Policy

SOLAR DOST  |  Effective Date: October 26, 2023

At SOLAR DOST, accessible from our mobile application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by SOLAR DOST and how we use it.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data from our users. Any information provided within the app for calculations remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Google Ads): Used to serve advertisements.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to monitor app performance.

3. Permissions

  • Internet: To load ads and verify solar panel brands online.
  • Storage: To save your calculation results on your device.

4. Data Storage & Deletion

All calculation data, saved results, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not maintain any external database of your personal information.

When you uninstall SOLAR DOST from your device, all locally stored data — including your calculation history, saved results, and preferences — is automatically and permanently deleted. No trace of this data is retained by us after uninstallation, since it was never uploaded to any server in the first place.

5. App Features

SOLAR DOST provides the following calculators and tools to help users plan and manage their solar power systems:

  • Load Calculator — Estimate your total electrical load requirement.
  • Battery Calculator — Calculate battery sizing and backup capacity.
  • Panel Calculator — Determine the number of solar panels needed.
  • Controller Calculator — Find the right solar charge controller (MPPT/PWM).
  • Inverter Calculator — Determine the correct inverter capacity.
  • Backup Calculator — Check how long your system will run on backup.
  • Problem Solver — Troubleshoot common solar system issues.
  • Safety Tips — Guidance on safety precautions for solar systems.
  • Savings Calculator — Estimate savings on your electricity bill.
  • Wire Calculator — Determine the correct wire size (AWG/mm).
  • Mixed Panel Calculator — Check compatibility of different panel types used together.
  • Panel Verification — Verify solar panel details and specifications.

These features perform all calculations locally on your device using the data you input, and this data is not shared with any third party.
